About SK Jewelry

SK Jewelry is a passionate artisan workshop based in Alexandria, Egypt. We specialize in creating unique, handcrafted jewelry inspired by the rich history and intricate designs of ancient Egyptian culture.

Our founder, Sarah Kamel, started this journey with a deep love for both jewelry making and Egyptology. Combining these passions, she created SK Jewelry to bring the mystique and elegance of pharaonic designs to modern-day fashion enthusiasts.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted using traditional techniques and high-quality materials. We pride ourselves on attention to detail and historical accuracy, ensuring that every item tells a story from Egypt's glorious past.

At SK Jewelry, we believe in preserving cultural heritage while creating contemporary pieces that resonate with today's fashion-forward individuals. Our goal is to offer not just jewelry, but wearable art that connects you to the timeless beauty of ancient Egypt.
